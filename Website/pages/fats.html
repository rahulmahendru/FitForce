Fats

Why are Fats important?

Act as an energy source
Essential for cell growth
Formation of hormones

How much Fats should I take?

Personally, I like to achieve 25-30% of my calories from fatty foods.

A good idea is to fill 1/4 of your plate with fats.

Myths about Fats

  • Myth: Fats make you gain weight.

    Truth: Eating balanced amounts of fats does not make you gain weight. On the contrary, essential amounts of fats aid in weight loss.

  • Myth: High fat foods are unhealthy.

    Truth: High consumption of foods high in saturated fats are unhealthy.
