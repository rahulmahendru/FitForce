Me talking about my passion!

I love to workout. I love to take care of my health and body. I love adventure and doing new things. I love food. Fitness incorporates all of these for me. My passion for fitness began in the Junior year of my undergraduate journey. I started learning about nutrition, weight loss and forms of workouts in general. I started working out and applying my knowledge to my own body and soon realized the right foods can do wonders for the human body. I have spent time performing Yoga, weight lifting and body weight training. I am currently using a mixed body weight and weight lifting form. I will soon shift to a more general CrossFit form, as I enjoy performance workouts better. Through this website, I want to share my knowledge and passion about fitness!

My mission for FitForce!

FitForce is a health and fitness website offering information, workout and eating tips and plans to fitness enthusiasts who want to gain knowledge about nutrition, weight loss and physical fitness. Unlike other fitness websites which are based on blogs promoting various lifestyles or paid workout plans, FitForce provides free information to spread health awareness and help beginners plan fitness routines.
