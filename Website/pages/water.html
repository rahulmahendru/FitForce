Water

Why is water important?

Human Body comprises of 50%-80% of water
Water is basis of blood and many body functions
Aids in digestion and blood flow
Forms part of bones and muscles.

How much water should I drink?

While there are different reccomendations based on age and gender, I personally aim for atleast 10 cups of water per day, which is approximately 2.5 litres.
